Our Company

Safekeep

Digital Asset

we specialize in digital estate planning, helping individuals and families secure their digital legacies for the future.

What we do

We understand that in today's digital age, our lives are intertwined with our online presence, from cherished memories on social media to valuable digital assets.

That's why we offer a comprehensive platform to assist you in organizing, preserving, and passing on your digital life seamlessly.

Our services include:
Digital Wasiat / Will Creation: Craft detailed digital wasiat / will that specify your wishes for your online accounts, assets, and digital presence.
Co-SAMPUL Guidance: Empower your chosen digital Co-SAMPUL with the tools and knowledge needed to carry out your wishes effectively.
Document Storage: Securely store important documents, passwords, and access information in one place, ensuring they're accessible when needed.
Digital Legacy Continuation: Preserve your digital memories and online identity, ensuring they live on for future generations.
Support and Resources: Access a wealth of resources, templates, and guides to simplify the digital estate planning process.

Who we are

SAMPUL is more than a platform; we're a team of dedicated individuals who are passionate about preserving the digital legacies of our users.

Our diverse team brings together expertise in digital security, estate planning, and technology to create a platform that's secure, user-friendly, and empowering.
Simplicity: We've designed SAMPUL to be user-friendly and intuitive, eliminating the complexities often associated with digital estate planning
Security: Your security is paramount to us. SAMPUL employs cutting-edge encryption and best-in-class security measures to keep your digital assets safe.
Comprehensive: SAMPUL covers all aspects of digital legacy planning, from social media accounts and digital assets to important documents.
